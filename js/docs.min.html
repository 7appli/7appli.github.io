sachin dev duggal

Co-founder & Chief Wizard

   

Hello you :) - I'm SDD – an entrepreneur, and an advocate of sustainable enterprise that makes money and solves a world problem; as the title suggests I am the co-founder and Chief Wizard at shoto after creating nivio with my best friend Saurabh Dhoot.

I am an investor and on the Board of Planet M (a large retail chain for music, movies and gaming), am an advisor to Eros International (Bollywood Studio, Distribution Company) and finally am working with Saurabh on creating the Sachin & Saurabh Foundation for educating children in impoverished areas around the world.

With great humility I was in the Class of 2009 with the World Economic Forum Technology Pioneers; my other accomplishments include MIT Innovator of the Year under 35 in 2011, Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Finalist, BBC Young Asian Achiever of the Year 2001, PWC Leader of Tomorrow in 2000.

I love tennis, meditation, singing, salsa and having a debate on solving education – secretly I love fast cars! Come give me a shout @sachinduggal

Oh and I always wanted to be a rebel in moderation :)

nick noreña

Co-founder and Chief Storyteller

Follow me here.

sandy climan

Board Member

   

Sandy Climan is a well-respected and successful media and motion picture industry executive, talent agent and producer, who currently serves as President of Entertainment Media Ventures, Inc., a media investment and strategic advisory company, focusing on innovative technologies. From 2007 to 2010, Mr. Climan served as CEO of 3ality Digital, where he built the company into a global leader in the development and commercialization of 3D technologies.

While a member of the senior management team at Creative Artists Agency (CAA), Mr. Climan represented talent including Robert De Niro, Robert Redford, Kevin Costner, Danny DeVito, and Michael Mann. As the founding head of CAA’s corporate practice, Mr. Climan helped guide Sony Corporation’s acquisition of Columbia Pictures, Matsushita Electric’s acquisition of MCA/Universal, Coca-Cola’s advertising revitalization, Credit Lyonnais’ restructuring of MGM, and the NYNEX, Bell Atlantic and Pacific Telesis video joint venture, Tele-TV. Mr. Climan served as Corporate Executive Vice President and President of Worldwide Business Development at Universal Studios, overseeing corporate international strategy and strategic marketing, including overseeing the Consumer Products, Home Video, Pay Television, and New Media operating divisions.

Mr. Climan also has been a successful Hollywood producer, including the first digital live-action 3D motion picture, “U2 3D,” the CBS series “Robbery Homicide Division,” and the Howard Hughes biographical feature film “The Aviator,” directed by Martin Scorsese and starring Leonardo DiCaprio, for which he was awarded a British Academy Award and a Golden Globe Award.

shashi reddy

Advisor, Founder and CEO @Case-Mate

   

Shashi Reddy is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Case-Mate, one of the world’s leading designers and manufacturers of premium quality cases for digital devices.

Born and raised in India, Shashi completed his Bachelors Degree in Mechanical Engineering and moved to the United States in 1995. He went on to receive his Masters Degree in Industrial Design from Auburn University in 1997. Shashi was a top performer at several prestigious companies including Home Depot, Racetrack Petroleum, and Accenture before leaving to ultimately pursue his dream of becoming an entrepreneur.

In 2002, Shashi used his savings to start his first business, a die casting plant in India that made components for US customers. He quickly realized that the emerging Indian economy had a major appetite for US brands, and he used this knowledge to his advantage. Shashi went on to setup a joint venture with Anheuser Busch (Now AB In-Bev) and successfully launched Budweiser Beer in India in 2005. Anheuser Busch has since bought the venture back.

By 2006, smart phones had a market penetration of less than 2%. Shashi, an avid gadget lover and early adaptor, realized that the demand for smart phones would grow exponentially, and seized the opportunity to create Case-Mate. Headquartered in Atlanta, GA, Case-Mate began with one simple goal - to make protective, stylish, and affordable cases for the gadgets people love. As CEO, Shashi’s passion, creativity and obsession with bringing the “next best thing,” to the market has translated into one of the world’s most innovative accessory companies. Case-Mate now has offices in 7 countries and sells products to major retailers in over 90 countries worldwide.
